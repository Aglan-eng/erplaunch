Questions

Answers first. Marketing second.

Which ERPs does ERPLaunch support?

First-party adaptors for NetSuite, SAP S/4HANA, Oracle Fusion, Microsoft Dynamics 365, Odoo, and ERPNext. A no-code Custom Adaptor wizard lets firms stand up any in-house or legacy system by uploading its analysis questionnaire and output templates.

Does ERPLaunch replace our implementation methodology?

No. Each ERP adaptor ships with its vendor-standard methodology (SAP Activate, SuiteSuccess, Oracle True Cloud Method, Microsoft's Implementation Guide, Odoo's, ERPNext playbook). Fork it, author your own; engagements tweak further. Provenance follows every element.

Can clients participate without logging in?

Yes. Clients answer wizard questions, upload files, and approve decisions by replying to email or WhatsApp. AI extracts structured answers; consultants review and accept. Voice notes are transcribed.

Is it white-label?

Every consulting firm operates ERPLaunch under their own brand — company name, logo, colors, and sending domain. The consultant workspace and the client portal inherit it.

What's the pricing?

Free during beta. Paid tiers launch with: per-engagement billing, per-consultant seat, and a marketplace revenue share for published verticals.
